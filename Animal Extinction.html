EXTINCT ANIMALS IN THE CURRENT YEAR

Number 1: Pinta Giant Tortoise

The Pinta giant tortoise (Chelonoidis abingdonii) was native to the Galapagos Islands. Lonesome George, the last known individual, died in captivity in 2012. The species faced extinction due to whalers using them as food and habitat destruction caused by introduced goats

Number 2: Splendid Poison Frog

The splendid poison frog (Oophaga speciosa) was declared extinct in 2020. It was last recorded in 1992. The chytrid fungus outbreak in its home range (western Cordillera Central in Panama) likely led to its extinction. Unfortunately, no living specimens exist in captivity1.

Number 4: Pyrenean Ibex

The Pyrenean ibex (Capra pyrenaica pyrenaica) was declared extinct in 2000. It is one of two extinct subspecies of the Spanish ibex. The exact cause of its extinction remains unknown
